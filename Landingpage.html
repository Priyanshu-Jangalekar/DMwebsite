Disaster Management App

An Android-based disaster response application built with Kotlin, Firebase, Google Maps API, and Gemini AI. Designed to provide real-time risk alerts, offline prediction, and emergency coordination.

⚠ Threshold-Based Prediction

Offline disaster alerts triggered when environmental parameters exceed defined risk limits.

🗺 Emergency Mapping

Highlights nearby hospitals, police stations, and fire services using Google Maps API.

🤖 AI Assistant

Integrated Gemini AI to answer disaster-related queries and provide guidance.

🚨 One-Click SOS

Instantly share live GPS location with emergency contacts during crises.
